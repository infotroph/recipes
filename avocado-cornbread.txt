Mix wet (big bowl):
1 c buttermilk

Melt together, add to wet:	
4 Tbsp butter (batter)

Chop chunks & mix:
1.5 c corn
1.5 jalapeno
1 avocado
0.5 lime juice

Add dry to wet, then fold in chunks. Turn oven down to 375, bake 30-40 min.
