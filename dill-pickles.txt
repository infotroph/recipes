Ma Plunkett's Dill Pickles

Brine for 8 qts pickles:
    1 quart vinegar
    3 quarts water
    1 cup salt

(The Utah Canning Guide suggests 2 lbs cukes/quart. )

Wash cukes twice. 
Have brine simmering while filling jars.
Each quart gets
    1 grape leaf
    2 heads dill
    2-3 cloves garlic
    2 teaspoons mustard seed
    As many cukes as will fit
    Brine to fill

Seal jars, put in boiling water, start timing immediately. Remove after 20 minutes.
